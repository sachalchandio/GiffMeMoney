Why it exists

Investing by hand asks you to be a desk of analysts.

Price, momentum, valuation, fundamentals, risk — each lives somewhere different and contradicts the others. GiffMeMoney does the synthesis, then shows you exactly how it got there.

  • ✕Emotion beats discipline. People buy euphoria and sell panic. A model doesn't flinch.
  • ✕The downside stays invisible. Almost nobody quantifies how much they could actually lose.
  • ✕No proof it ever worked. Without backtests, a “strategy” is just a story.
